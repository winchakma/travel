Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about car hire

  What documents do I need to hire a car?

You'll need a valid driving licence, passport or ID, and a credit card in the driver's name.

  Can I cancel my car hire booking?

Yes, most bookings can be cancelled for free up to 48 hours before pick-up.

  Is insurance included in the price?

Basic insurance is included. You can add extra coverage during checkout for full peace of mind.

  What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age is usually 21, though some suppliers accept drivers from age 18 with a young driver fee.
