Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate globetrotters, GoTrip began with a simple idea: booking a dream vacation shouldn't be a nightmare of open tabs and hidden fees.

What started as a small booking portal has now grown into a global ecosystem connecting millions of travelers with the finest hotels, tours, and experiences. We hand-pick our partners and relentlessly innovate to ensure every journey you take is perfect.
